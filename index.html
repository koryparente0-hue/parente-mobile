About Us

A Family Built on Honest Work

Parente Mobile Mechanics is a father and son team — Brian and Kory Parente — based right here in Wheaton, IL.

Brian spent years working at Toyota, so when he says he knows cars, he means it. After seeing friends and neighbors get overcharged at dealerships and chain shops — sometimes by the very industry he worked in — he decided there was a better way.

Together, Brian and Kory bring that dealership-level expertise directly to your driveway. No waiting rooms, no upsells, no runaround. Just honest mechanical work from people who live in your community and care about their reputation.

Our Services

What We Do

Preventive Maintenance Checkup

We come to your driveway and do a full inspection — brakes, fluids, belts, filters, lights, and more. Catch problems before they become expensive.

Before Road-Trip Checkup

Heading out of town? We make sure your car is road-ready before you leave — tires, fluids, brakes, and everything in between.
